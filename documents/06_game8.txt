TIP: Practice the Hit and Run Tactic
General combat in Silksong revolves around a hit-and-run tactic: hit the enemy at least
once, then immediately move away to reposition. Beginners should practice this on regular
mobs until they get used to the rhythm of combat. This applies to most encounters, normal
enemies and bosses alike. Staying alive matters more than dealing damage, and practicing
caution even in the early game helps with harder challenges later.
 
 
TIP: Run Into Walls and Open Every Switch
When exploring, check every wall and open every switch for possible secrets. Running into
walls can reveal hidden rooms containing items, secret paths, or Rosaries, and opening
switches usually removes exploration obstacles like locked gates and elevators. If
something can break, it is probably meant to break; this often applies to walls and vines
that open shortcuts, which is helpful when revisiting an area later.
 
 
TIP: Heal or Bind Only When Necessary
Binding (healing) fully depletes your Silk. While useful, it leaves you vulnerable because
you are trading health for ability use until Silk recharges. Since the early game has few
uses for Silk, use it only when necessary or when you can quickly replenish it. You can
also be interrupted while Binding: an interruption cancels the heal but still fully
depletes the Silk. Find a safe spot or moment in combat where completing a Bind is
guaranteed.
 
 
TIP: Use the Cocoon as Emergency Silk
When Hornet dies, she leaves behind a Cocoon at her death location and respawns at the
last bench rested on. Breaking this Cocoon restores Hornet's Silk (and the Rosaries lost
from the previous death) for healing or abilities. Instead of breaking it immediately,
treat the Cocoon as an extra spool of Silk. This is especially useful in later boss
encounters, where the extra Silk can provide an emergency heal or ability use.
 
 
TIP: Use Rosary Strings to Avoid Losing Rosaries
Rosaries are the main currency in Silksong and are lost on death. You can avoid losing
them by using Rosary Strings collected in Pharloom, or by paying a fee to certain vendors
to secure Rosaries to a string. Strung Rosaries cannot be spent without breaking the
string, so use this when expecting long expeditions without frequent vendor visits, or
when tackling unknown areas or difficult platforming sections.
 
 
TIP: Acquire the Maps ASAP
Getting an area's Map as soon as possible is a necessity when exploring somewhere new.
Locate Shakra when entering a new area and purchase all the map necessities to keep track
of your progress.
