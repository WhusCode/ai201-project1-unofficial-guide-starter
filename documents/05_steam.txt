The Hunter's Journal is given by Nuu in Greymoor (Halfway Home). There are 237 total
entries. Most enemies respawn when you sit on a bench or restart the game, except for
bosses and special enemies. To earn the first reward you must defeat 100 different
enemies. The entries for Shakra, Garmond & Zaza, Palestag, Lost Garmond, Lost Lace, and
Summoned Saviour are NOT required for the True Hunter achievement (the Summoned Saviour
is Steel Soul Mode only). After completing all required entries, talk to Nuu again to
receive the Hunter's Memento and unlock the achievement.

Entries marked with a note have a special spawn or unlock condition.
