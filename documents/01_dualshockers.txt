OVERVIEW

Hollow Knight: Silksong is a Metroidvania and Soulslike that has been very popular since launch. Like the original Hollow Knight, it is not an easy game: combat can be brutal, areas are vast and full of secrets, and platforming demands precision that many similar titles avoid. This page is a directory of guides covering boss strategies, area progression walkthroughs, side quest explanations, item locations, and more, intended to help players progress toward The Citadel and beyond. It is described as a work in progress that is updated regularly.
